Sci-Fi

My introduction to Sci-fi was through classics like 20,000 leagues under the sea and young adult fiction series like The Stainless Steel Rat by Harry Harrison and the Legionary books by Dougals Hill.

There are a lot of different genres within Sci-Fi, it’s not one thing, so whether you like epic, grand stories like Star Wars and the Ian M Banks culture novels; to dystopian books like Blade Runner (called ‘Do androids dream of electric sleep’ in the Philip K Dick original), Neuromancer or even the crime noire influenced Altered Carbon (which is fantastic) by Richard Morgan. Whether you love spaceships, near future speculation or hard technical novels, there’s a lot to choose from.
