Our Tragic Universe by Scarlett Thomas

This is the second Scarlett Thomas novel I’ve read, although it was the third I tried to read. It was similar to ‘End of Mr Y’, in that it melded together academia and great characters, but different enough that I really enjoyed it and wouldn’t consider the books similar.
