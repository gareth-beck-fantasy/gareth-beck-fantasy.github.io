Magical Realism book reviews

I’ve read a fair few books around this including Neil Gaiman, Haruki Marakami and Scarlett Thomas. I think more than other genres there seems to be more diversity here. There isn’t a lot to tie them together but loosely they all contain elements of the supernatural that appear normal.
