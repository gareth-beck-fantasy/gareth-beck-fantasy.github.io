War of Kings, Part 1 by Brandon Sanderson

This is the first part of a two part book in the Stormlight archive. I’d read the Mistborn Trilogy and really liked that so thought this might be good, and it was.

I love Brandons world building and the effortless way that the systems of magic work. It’s a great read and I’m now looking for part 2. WHSmiths has a combined edition and I wasn’t that bothered about getting that. I’d happily buy all of these in hardback if I could but I don’t have room.

The first book follows several characters, one of which falls from grace and it hints of something else going on that will be revealed. It’s not a short read and the fact that both books 1 and 2 are in split in two is both exciting and slightly daunting, it’s a big investment of time and I, for one, hope that the editing has been good.

Feel free to share!
