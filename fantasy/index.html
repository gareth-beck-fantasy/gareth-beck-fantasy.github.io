Fantasy literature reviews

Fantasy is a huge genre, so no matter what you’re looking for, you’re likely to find it.

From the themes of love and companionship found in the Epic Fantasy of JRR Tolkien; to the black and white, good vs bad themes found in the Heroic Fantasy of David Gemmell, the grittier themes that are in Dark Fantasy, (also called Grimdark Fantasy) of Joe Abercrombie and George RR Martin (you might be aware of the TV series). There’s a lot to choose from.

I’ve been reading fantasy novels for 25 years and thought I’d collect my reviews here. I hope you enjoy them.
