Old Mans War by John Scalzi

This was recommended via Twitter and having followed JS for a while it seemed like as good an opportunity as any.

Briefly, Old Mans War is about a 75 yr old guy who joins the colonial army with the promise of being young again.

It’s a great read and morally complex, it resists the temptation of putting people (or species) into ‘the other’, and instead shows good and bad across both.

Generally the tone is light but with the occasional dive into physics and maths that I think I understood while I was reading it, but now I’m not so sure.

It’s the first of five books and I can see myself reading the others before too long as well.

Feel free to share!
