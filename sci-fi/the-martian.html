The Martian by Andrew Weir

In this situation I would have been a mess. I would have spent a few days crying, then got drunk, then died. Not this guy though. He man’s up, and not wanting to spoil anything, has more problems than you would think probable.

I enjoyed it a lot and shows that detailed, plausible Sci Fi works best when routed in strong characters.

Hugely entertaining.

It’s going to be interesting to see what kind of a mess the film makes of this [note: they didn’t, it was great].
