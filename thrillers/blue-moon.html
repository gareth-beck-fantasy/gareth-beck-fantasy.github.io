Jack Reacher 24 - Blue Moon by Lee Childs

Having asked for it for Christmas I bought this one shortly after.

There’s more characters in Blue Moon than in a lot of the other books, which is good, but I’m not sure that all their reactions stack up.

There’s more description of music than I’ve read on these in ages and the plot makes sense.

It’s interesting that it’s a plot within a plot in this one. It feels more complex than the last two and I really enjoyed it.

Fake news [fine, that might be a spoiler!] is prominent and is used to good affect.

It also shows how utterly devastating not having an NHS is: no-one should need to go to loan sharks for money for treatment and it’s nauseating how believable it is that they had no other choice.

Just a year to wait for the next one!
