Thrillers

The thriller genre is hugely successful and one that I’d not been reading until more recently. I think that one of the first ones I read would have been one of the Lee Childs Jack Reacher novels, I can’t remember which one but I do know that I’ve since read all 22 of them (unless that includes the short story anthologies, in which case I’ve read most of them).

There is something very appealing about thrillers and Lee Childs and Harlen Coben both excel in writing character driven stories that use great writing to create real page turners.
